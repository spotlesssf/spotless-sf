01

Information We Collect

When you book a service, contact us, or visit our website, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, and phone number
  • Service address: The address where cleaning services will be performed
  • Payment information: Credit or debit card details, processed securely through our payment processor
  • Booking details: Service type, preferred dates and times, and any special instructions
  • Communications: Messages or inquiries you send to us via email, phone, or our website
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent, collected via analytics tools
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To schedule, confirm, and provide cleaning services
  • To process payments and send invoices
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up messages
  • To respond to your questions or requests
  • To improve our services and website experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will not use your information for any purpose not described in this policy without your prior consent.

03

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business, including:

  • Jobber: Our booking and client management platform, used to schedule appointments, send communications, and process invoices
  • Payment processors: To securely handle credit and debit card transactions
  • Google: For website analytics and business tools

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Spotless SF, our clients, or others.

04

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This data is collected anonymously and helps us improve our website experience.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website.

05

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you would like us to delete your information, please contact us and we will do so in accordance with applicable law.

06

Your Rights Under California Law (CCPA)

As a California resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know: You may request details about what personal information we have collected about you and how it is used
  • Right to delete: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to opt out: We do not sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of in that regard
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@spotlesssf.com. We will respond to your request within 45 days as required by California law.

07

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Payment information is processed through secure, encrypted channels and is never stored directly on our systems.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as our booking platform or social media pages. This Privacy Policy applies only to spotlesssf.com. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

09

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
